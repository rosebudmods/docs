## available colours

rainglow's available colours are Minecraft's default 16, and `indigo`:

- `black`
- `blue`
- `brown`
- `cyan`
- `gray`
- `green`
- `indigo`
- `light_blue`
- `light_gray`
- `lime`
- `magenta`
- `orange`
- `pink`
- `purple`
- `red`
- `white`
- `yellow`
